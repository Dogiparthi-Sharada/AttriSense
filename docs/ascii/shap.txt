SHAP Explanation — How a Prediction is Decomposed (ASCII)
==========================================================

Every per-employee prediction sums to:
   baseline + sum(feature contributions)

Example for EMP_2417 (final risk = 0.83):

  baseline (avg risk for everyone)              0.32  ████████
  + short tenure (7 months)                    +0.21  █████
  + low salary (52k vs 78k median)             +0.18  ████
  + Manufacturing dept                         +0.09  ██
  + new manager (4 months)                     +0.03  █
  ---------------------------------------------------
  final risk for this employee                  0.83  ████████████████████

Property guaranteed by SHAP:
  the bars above always sum to (final - baseline)

What SHAP says:
  "this prediction depended on these features"
What SHAP does NOT say:
  "these features cause attrition"
For causal questions use the T-Learner uplift module instead.
